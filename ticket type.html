Each ticket includes specific benefits so you know what you get: Regular gives standard access, VIP gives extra perks, and Student gives entry plus a meal coupon with valid ID.



A Regular ticket allows entry to the main event area and access to all standard activities. With this ticket, you can enjoy the performances, visit the stalls, and take part in the open games. It includes one free drink coupon.

A VIP ticket gives you premium benefits, including front-row seating, access to the VIP lounge, and complimentary snacks and drinks. You will also receive a special gift bag and have the chance to meet the performers after the event.

A Student ticket is a budget-friendly option for those with a valid student ID. It allows entry to all general activities, and you will also get a free meal coupon that can be used to get snacks or drinks from the food stalls.
